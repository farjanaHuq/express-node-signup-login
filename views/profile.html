About Me

This is Farjana Huq. I am a creative developer, a daughter, a wife, a mom of two wonderful kids, and I love coding, creating dynamic and interactive web pages. I look at the problems as puzzles, try them and have a lot of fun solving them. I enjoy discussing the programming challenge or topics with my peers and believe in team spirits.

I was born and raised in Bangladesh. In Bangladesh, people are used to seeing the women as a teacher, a doctor or mostly a homemaker, but I wanted to become an engineer from my childhood. Later, I completed my bachelor in Mechanical Engineering in 2010. After my bachelor, I went to Germany with my husband and studied masters in Sensor System Technology. At the end of my Masters, during my master thesis, I developed a concept of an application called ‘Image Processing of Particles in Water’, which I coded with Java. That was the beginning of my software development journey, gradually my love for coding kept increasing.

My first kid was born right after my Master in 2014, so took time off for him about a year and a half, during that time learned C++ myself. Later, I worked as a C++ Software Developer in Germany. I left my job in June 2017, moved to the USA. My second child was born here, so I decided to spend a year with her again, and I used to study and create Swift based application while she felt asleep. In April 2019, I went to the UCI Division of Continuing Education for Full Stack Developer Bootcamp. I have built quite a few full-stack applications afterwards which you will see in the Project section. In 2020, I started working as Cloud Integration Developer.
